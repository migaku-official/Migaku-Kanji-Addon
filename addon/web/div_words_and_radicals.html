Vocab

Colored buttons are vocab from cards you've learned. Outlined buttons are new cards that you haven't learned (yet). Grey buttons are clickable dictionary examples.
Shift + click for dictionary lookup.
Vocab buttons show your linked cards on desktop. On mobile, they search jisho.org as a compromise.

Primitives

The largest parts we can break this kanji down into. Primitives are usually kanji in their own right, but some only exist to help with mnemonic stories.

Radicals

Radicals are used to organize kanji in dictionaries. They're generally the smallest parts a kanji can be broken down into.
Radicals aren't clickable (yet).
